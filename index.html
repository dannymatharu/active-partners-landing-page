What the dashboard gives your Active Partnership

Six distinct uses for the data your area generates automatically.

🗺️
Area-wide social value picture
Total social value, social value ratios, health and productivity savings and volunteer contribution aggregated across every club in your area. The granular evidence Sport England wants underneath the area aggregate — which clubs, which communities, which populations.
📍
Place-based data your leads can use
IMD deprivation decile per club postcode, automatically sourced from government data. Your place leads see which clubs are in the most deprived communities, which are eligible for targeted funding, and where the case for investment is strongest.
💷
Funding readiness across your network
The dashboard flags which clubs are ready for which specific funding pots — Sport England, National Lottery, local authority grants, Barclays — based on each club's own data. You become the organisation that helped them get funded.
🏥
Health & productivity savings evidence for ICS conversations
Healthcare savings estimates aggregated across your whole network, built on HM Treasury WELLBY methodology. The language public health commissioners and Integrated Care Systems already use — ready for your next meeting.
🧠
Hidden welfare need by area
ND and MH prevalence estimates across your whole club network — statistically, not case-by-case. How many players across your area are likely to need additional support? Now you can answer that question.
📋
Quarterly reports, automatically
The dashboard generates quarterly PDF reports for your board, Sport England, local authorities, and ICS presentations. Export CSV at any point. Multi-sport breakdown included — not just football.

Sport England reporting

A different conversation
with the people holding the budget.

Sport England's Active Lives data tells you participation numbers. Their area-level social value model gives you the aggregate. But neither tells you which specific clubs and communities are generating that value — and that's the question that matters when you're in a room with a local authority or a public health commissioner deciding where to invest.

Proof of Play gives you the granular layer. Which clubs in your area are generating the strongest social value? Which communities have the highest deprivation and the lowest investment? Which populations are being reached?

That's a fundamentally different report to put in front of Sport England than an area aggregate. And a fundamentally different conversation with a council deciding whether to renew facility leases.

Without Proof of Play

"Our area generates £Xbn in social value from community sport and physical activity."

With Proof of Play

"Our area generates £Xbn — and here's the breakdown by club, community and deprivation decile. These twelve clubs in your most deprived wards generate £Y in combined social value and £Z in health and productivity savings. This is what cutting their facility budgets actually costs."

Public health commissioners

Community sport as a
public health investment case.

Public health commissioners and Integrated Care Systems want to see community sport framed as a health intervention, not just a participation programme. They speak the language of health savings, WELLBY methodology, and cost per outcome.

Every club report generates a health and productivity savings estimate using HM Treasury WELLBY methodology. Aggregate those across your network and you have a public health investment case built from the ground up — in the language commissioners already use.

Add the deprivation context and population breakdown, and the conversation changes completely.

How it works

Simple to deploy.
Your place leads do the rest.

01 · You

One licence. Your whole area.

A single annual licence covers every club across your Active Partnership area. Multi-sport by design — football, cricket, rugby, swimming, bowls. Your place leads distribute it as a service from your organisation. The relationship stays yours.

02 · Clubs

Ten minutes. A full report.

Clubs enter their own data — players, volunteers, sessions, programmes. The tool applies published proxy values and generates a complete social value report immediately. No support needed, no training required.

03 · You again

The area picture builds automatically.

Every completion feeds your dashboard — aggregate value, deprivation data by place, health savings, funding readiness, welfare infrastructure. Quarterly reports generated automatically for your Sport England, local authority, and ICS conversations.
